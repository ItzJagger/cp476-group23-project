Echo Ward: Silent Descent

Echo Ward: Silent Descent is a psychological horror experience set in a long-abandoned hospital filled with disturbing secrets.

Solve environmental puzzles, avoid unseen threats, and piece together what really happened through scattered clues and recordings.

The deeper you go, the more reality begins to distort — and not everything you see can be trusted.
